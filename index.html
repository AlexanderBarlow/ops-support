Catering Ops Support

Help & troubleshooting for the Catering Ops mobile app.

Internal business app • Access controlled by administrators

Who this app is for

Catering Ops is intended for authorized catering staff and managers. Accounts are created and managed by an administrator—there is no public sign-up.

Need help?

If you can’t sign in, don’t see orders, or something looks off, contact your catering administrator or store operator for access and troubleshooting.

  • Login issues Confirm you’re using the correct email and password. If your account was recently created or reset, ask your administrator to resend credentials.
  • Orders not appearing Pull down to refresh. Orders display based on pickup/scheduled time and the current business day. If it’s still empty, verify the source system is ingesting new orders.
  • Status updates / swipes Order status is updated via swipe actions on the Today screen. If the wrong card was updated, contact your administrator to adjust status or follow your store’s correction workflow.
  • Contact For support, reach out to your catering administrator. If you maintain this app, you can link a support email here later (optional).
